SO MUCH TO LEARN!

I got interested in web development during the height of covid. During those times when we couldn't leave the house I found The Odin Project. The subject matter was interesting and the projects kept me busy for hours. Two examples of such projects can be found below. We all went back to work eventually but I couldn't stop thinking about how much I enjoyed building web apps and writing code. Fast forward some time and now I am in school figuring out how to make this a career. I will be updating my work samples here as I go. Stay tuned.

Calculator

Does what is says on the box. This was my first attempt at making a browser based app that uses the DOM and JS to make an interactive calculator. It was a great lesson on OOP and I had a lot of fun (and maybe a little head ache) figuring it out.

Draw Pad

This draw pad project really challenged me to think inside the box. Figuring out how to use JS to reactively make the HTML grid based on user input was no small task at that point in my learning. It might not be pretty but I am proud of it.
